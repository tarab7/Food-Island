ABOUT US

Food Island is a day Restaurant and night Bar with food and drink! Classic bistro ideas and Southern ingredients are combined
in a new space with an open kitchen and a full bar & raw bar. Since opening in 2005, Food Island has evolved continually, and this most
recent evolution has culminated in its relocation and re-imagining into a new space in the West Village. Here, we're constantly
pushing ourselves to find new ways to redefine the dining experience and we are wholeheartedly embracing small shareable plates and
large shareable meats, with house-made pasta and all the oysters and raw bar ingredients we can find.
